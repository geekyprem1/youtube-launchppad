## 6. Core Modules — Feature Specifications

### MODULE 1: AI YouTube Growth Coach (Daily Action Plan)
**Addresses:** Pain Point #4 ("Mujhe roz koi guide kare")
- **Dashboard:** A personalized daily feed of tasks.
- **Tasks:** Next upload suggestion, keyword advice, thumbnail improvements.
- **Alerts:** Trend alerts and competitor insights summarized daily.
- **Analytics:** Actionable recommendations instead of just graphs.

### MODULE 2: Next Video Recommendation Engine
**Addresses:** Pain Point #1 ("Meri next video kya honi chahiye?")
- **Inputs:** Analyzes channel data, audience demographics, competitors, and trends.
- **Output:** Recommends top personalized video ideas with the highest growth potential.

### MODULE 3: Diagnostics Tool ("Why My Videos Are Not Getting Views?")
**Addresses:** Pain Point #2 ("Views kyu nahi aa rahe?")
- **Features:** Diagnoses thumbnail, title, hook, retention drop-offs, SEO, and audience mismatch.
- **Output:** Provides actionable fixes instead of raw analytics.

### MODULE 4: Video Success Predictor
**Addresses:** Pain Point #3 ("Upload se pehle bata do video chalegi ya nahi")
- **Features:** Predict CTR, retention, topic demand, and competition before publishing.
- **Output:** Estimated success probability score.

### MODULE 5: Competitor Intelligence
**Addresses:** Pain Point #5 ("Competitors kya kar rahe hain...")
- **Features:** Track competitors, detect viral topics, identify content gaps, monitor upload patterns, and surface growth opportunities.
